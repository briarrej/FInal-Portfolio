Chicago: The Black Experience

Chicago is a melting pot for multiple cultures, but the culture of Black people in Chicago is one that is important and extremely personal. There are so many organizations, restaurants, and events ran by Black people that have a positive impact on the community.


About Me

Hi Everyone! My name is Briarre Johnson and I am a Chicago native, also known as a Chicagoan! Chicago is very important to me because I am Chicago. My roots reside in the city of Chicago and I take pride in the amazing things we have going on in the city. If you would like to know more or get involved in the city of Chicago, please feel free to contact me through the form below!
